DUYA Browser Bridge

Privacy Policy · Last updated: 2025

Overview

DUYA Browser Bridge ("the Extension") is a browser automation tool that works in conjunction with the DUYA Desktop application. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or usage data to external servers.

All browser automation operations occur locally on your device. The Extension communicates exclusively with the DUYA Desktop application running on the same machine via a local WebSocket connection (ws://127.0.0.1:19825).

Permissions Used

  • debugger – Required to control browser tabs via Chrome DevTools Protocol for automation tasks.
  • tabs / activeTab – Required to identify and manage browser tabs for navigation and screenshot capture.
  • storage – Used locally to store your blocked domains list; no data leaves your device.
  • alarms / background – Required to maintain the service worker for WebSocket reconnection and automation heartbeat.
  • <all_urls> – Required to automate any website you choose to control. The Extension only operates on pages you explicitly ask the agent to interact with.

Local Data Storage

The Extension stores the following locally in your browser:

  • Blocked domains list (configured by you in Settings)
  • Internal connection state and reconnection preferences

No data is synced across devices or browsers.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. It does not include any third-party code that could access your data.

Data Sharing

The Extension does not share any data with any third parties. All communication is confined to the local DUYA Desktop application on your device.

Security

All browser automation is performed in an isolated automation window to protect your active browsing session. The Extension cannot access tabs outside this isolated environment.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
